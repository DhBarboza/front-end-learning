STARSET

The band Starset is an innovative blend of alternative rock and electronic elements hailing from Columbus, Ohio. Fronted by the charismatic vocalist Dustin Bates, the group is distinguished by its futuristic visual aesthetic and a cosmic narrative that permeates its lyrics. Incorporating elements of electronic, orchestral, and intense rock music, Starset creates a unique and engaging sonic experience. Their live performances are known for being energetic and visually impactful, transporting the audience to a fascinating and unknown world. The band doesn't just deliver music but a sensory experience that challenges conventional boundaries and captivates listeners with an emotional and imaginative journey.

Origin

The band Starset was formed in 2013 in Columbus, Ohio, by vocalist and songwriter Dustin Bates. Dustin, who holds a degree in electrical engineering and a Ph.D. in aerospace engineering, fused his passions for science and music to create a unique artistic proposition.

The band's debut album, "Transmissions," was released in 2014, featuring an innovative blend of alternative rock, electronic music, and cinematic elements. The album includes hits like "My Demons" and "Carnivore," which helped establish Starset in the music scene.

The concept behind Starset goes beyond music, involving a fictional and futuristic narrative. The band crafted a story about an organization called "The Starset Society," aiming to disseminate knowledge about science and explore the mysteries of the universe. This narrative inspired both the band's visual aesthetic and the lyrics of their songs.

With a unique approach to music and a fusion of scientific, fictional, and artistic elements, Starset continues to garner fans worldwide, solidifying their position as one of the most original and intriguing bands in contemporary music.
